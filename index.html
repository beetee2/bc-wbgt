KHSAA Practice Guidelines (Form GE20)

Guidelines from KHSAA Form GE20 (Rev. 06/23). Temperature, humidity, and wind come from the Breckinridge County Airport weather station in Hardinsburg (KI93, ~5 mi from Harned), updated every 10–20 minutes; solar radiation and the hourly forecast come from the Open-Meteo model, which is also the fallback if the station is offline. WBGT is estimated from those readings — an estimate is not a substitute for an on-field WBGT meter — when in doubt, err on the side of caution.
